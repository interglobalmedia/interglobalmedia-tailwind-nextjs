## Description

### User Story

I can **_search_** `Wikipedia` **_entries_** in a `search box` and **_see_** the **_resulting_** `Wikipedia` **_entries_**.

I can click a button to see a **_random_** `Wikipedia` **_entry_**.

This is a **_refactor_** of a `project` I **_created_** several years ago. I **_revamped_** the `workflow`, **_migrated_** from `Gulp` to `Webpack`, from `jQuery` to `Modern JavaScript` with `async await`, `@babel/pollyfill` to **_make_** my `async await code` **_work_** with `Babel 7` and `Webpack 5`, **_adding_** `css animated scroll` **_buttons_**, **_animated_** `magnifying glass icon search input` on `focus`, `hover`, and `active`, **_replacing_** `PostCSS` with `Node Sass`, and **_adding_** a `dynamic year` in the `footer`.
