## Description

An app which **_creates_** a `custom HTML5 video player` **_using_** the `Shadow DOM` so that the `video player` can go `full screen` **_without issue_**. This `custom HTML5 video player` looks **_more like_** a `YouTube` or `Netflix` **_experience_** instead of a user agent stylesheet!

To **_read_** my `post` on my **_experience building_** this `application`, please visit [Creating a Custom HTML5 Video Player And The Shadow DOM](https://www.interglobalmedianetwork.com/blog/2017-01-04-creating-a-custom-html5-video-player-and-the-shadow-dom/).
